Project Overview

PlumDM Auto is a service that allows clients to select, customize, and build a landing page completely on their own. The idea was to create a service that could provide free or extremely low cost landing pages without tying up the time of the marketing company’s developers.

The client has the ability to select from a variety of different templates, then choose a custom color scheme and customize most of the page’s information and copy. They then can submit the completed page, which creates a .zip file of all landing page HTML and CSS which is automatically sent to web developer. The developer than only has to copy this folder to the server, essentially automating the entire landing page creation process.
